Welcome to Brook Cottage Camping

    Tranquil, intimate spot on a working farm amid the County Down drumlins

    Just a 10‑minute drive to Drumkeeragh Forest and 30 minutes from Belfast

    Shops and dining in Ballynahinch are only a 15‑minute drive away

    Craving peace near forests, beaches, and city life? Brook Cottage Camping nestled in the gentle drumlins of County Down is your perfect retreat. You’re only a 10-minute drive from Drumkeeragh Forest—ideal for scenic walks—and just thirty minutes from Belfast, Northern Ireland’s vibrant capital.

    The small, family-friendly camping area is a pop-up site set on a working farm, offering portable toilets and a washing-up spot. Situated on a quiet country lane, you won’t have much traffic noise, yet it’s easy to head off for day trips to Northern Ireland’s top attractions.

    Grab your fishing gear and hit one of the nearby lakes—just a 15-minute drive away—or stock up on provisions in the market town of Ballynahinch, with all the shops and eateries you’d expect.

    In a 20-minute drive through Dromara and Finnis you’ll reach Slieve Croob, the birthplace of the River Lagan. After your climb, you’ll be treated to expansive views. And if you fancy an indoor treat afterward, Lisburn, 25 minutes away, has a pool with slides, a cinema, and bowling.
